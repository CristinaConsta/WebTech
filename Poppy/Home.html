Our newest happy stories



New born kittens found on Main road

Last week, on Main Road, our volunteers have found a big box with new born kittens.

There were starved, dehydrated and very frightened, but they trusted our people and accepted the help.

Back to our shelter, the kittens were fed, cared for, and vaccinated. Now all of them are in good health and waiting to be adopted.

Big donation received

As always, we rely on donations offered by good people in our community, as well as on our long-time partners.

Yesterday we had a big surprise, receiving a substantial pet food donation from a loving family, which wanted to remain anonymous. They have adopted a puppy last year and on every one of his birthdays, they make a substantial donation to our shelter.

Many thanks from us and especially from our little friends!

The surgery of Max was a success!

Max, the dog we have rescued 3 weeks ago, with left leg injuries, successfully underwent foot surgery. We are very happy to thank our vet team members, lovely and dedicated people!
